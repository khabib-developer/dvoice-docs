Welcome to the DVoice documentation! This guide serves as your starting point to explore the capabilities of the DVoice platform, which provides advanced text-to-speech (TTS) and speech-to-text (STT) services. Whether you're a developer looking to integrate our APIs or a user seeking to understand our offerings, this documentation will help you get started.

## Getting Started

Begin your journey with DVoice by exploring the following sections:

- [Authentication](./authentication) to secure your API access.
- [Models](./models) to learn about available voice and language options.

</Callout>
