The DVoice platform offers a range of models for text-to-speech (TTS) and speech-to-text (STT) functionalities. Below, you can explore the available models and their intended uses.

## Text-to-Speech (TTS) Models

DVoice provides two TTS models, each designed to deliver natural-sounding speech:

- **Surayyo_v2**: An enhanced version of the Surayyo model, featuring improved voice clarity, expressiveness, and support for emotional voice tones. Perfect for applications needing dynamic and engaging audio output.
- **Iroda**: A versatile TTS model with a distinct voice profile, suitable for a variety of use cases from casual to professional settings.

## Speech-to-Text (STT) Models

DVoice currently supports one STT model, optimized for accurate transcription of spoken language:

- **STT Model**: This model is designed to convert speech into text with reliable accuracy. Further details on its performance and supported languages will be available as it matures.
